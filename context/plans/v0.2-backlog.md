### 7. cc-hook real `include_bytes!` embedding — DONE (via cargo-install sidestep)
**Source:** `crates/cli/build.rs` + `extensions/claude-code/src/lib.rs` — `CC_HOOK_BYTES: &[u8] = &[]` (stub).
**Flagged by:** claude-code-ext Tier 1 T-008a (ledger).

**Fix delivered:** Took the sidestep option — `install_cc_hook_at` now falls through to `cargo install --bin cc-hook --path <extensions/claude-code> --root <install-root> --locked` when `CC_HOOK_BYTES` is the empty stub.
